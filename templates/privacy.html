Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information including your name, email, phone number, National ID, region, and KYC documents. We also collect transaction data, login activity, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to: process loan applications, verify identity through KYC, calculate credit scores, send notifications, comply with legal obligations, and improve our services.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with: regulatory authorities as required by law, service providers who assist in platform operations, and partners with your explicit consent.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (bcrypt for passwords, TLS for data in transit) and secure storage practices. Your KYC documents are stored securely with restricted access.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Audit logs and transaction records are retained for regulatory compliance. You may request account deletion at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to: access your personal data, correct inaccurate information, request data deletion, opt out of marketing communications, and export your transaction history.

7. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us through the Support section of the platform.
