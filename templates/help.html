Frequently asked questions and guides

YoCoin is a blockchain-powered microloan platform for Uganda. We provide collateral-free loans to help individuals grow their businesses, with transparent terms and a credit scoring system that rewards good repayment behavior.

1. Register an account with your National ID. 2. Complete KYC verification by uploading required documents. 3. Once verified, go to the Loans page and fill out the application form. 4. Wait for admin approval (typically within 24 hours).

Loan amounts range from 50,000 to 1,000,000 UGX. Interest rates depend on your credit score: Excellent (750+): 3%, Good (700-749): 3.5%, Fair (650-699): 5%, Poor (600-649): 7.5%, Very Poor (<600): 10%.

Your credit score (300-850) is based on: Payment History (35%), Credit Utilization (30%), Length of Credit History (15%), Loan Diversity (10%), and Recent Applications (10%). Timely repayments increase your score, while defaults decrease it.

You have a 7-day grace period after the due date. If payment is not made within this period, your loan will be marked as defaulted, late fees may apply, and your credit score will be negatively impacted.

Share your unique referral code with friends. When they register using your code and complete their first loan, you receive a reward of 5,000 UGX in your account. There is no limit to how many people you can refer.

After registration, check your email for a verification link. Click the link to verify your account. If you didn't receive it, go to your dashboard and click "Resend verification" or contact support.
